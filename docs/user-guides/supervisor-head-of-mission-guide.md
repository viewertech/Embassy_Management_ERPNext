# Consular Supervisor And Head Of Mission Guide

[Powered by Viewertech](https://viewertech.net)

This guide is for consular supervisors, heads of consular section, and heads of mission.

## Oversight Role

Use the Embassy Management workspace for operational visibility, approvals, escalations, and performance reporting.

Supervisors should review:

- Pending officer review
- Overdue cases
- Urgent and emergency cases
- Rejection reasons
- Officer workload
- Document rejection summary
- Appointment no-shows
- Revenue summaries

## Review Escalated Cases

Open:

```text
Consular Application
```

Filter by:

- Assigned officer
- Service
- Priority
- Application status
- Review status

For escalated cases, review internal notes, applicant communications, uploaded documents, payment state, and officer recommendation.

## Approvals And Decisions

Workflows are configurable per service. Depending on mission policy, supervisors may approve:

- Final visa decision
- Passport service decision
- Attestation or legalisation output
- Fee waiver
- Emergency processing
- Exceptional appointment handling

Heads of mission may have read-only executive dashboard access and approval access where configured.

## Dashboard Review

Use dashboard cards and reports to monitor:

- Total applications today
- Pending review
- Pending payments
- Appointments today
- Approved this week
- Rejected this week
- Revenue this month
- Average processing time
- Urgent cases
- Overdue cases

Use report exports only according to data protection policy.

## Governance Notes

Do not store official flags, coats of arms, or protected emblems in the Git repository. Upload approved assets through ERPNext after client approval.

Ensure privacy notice, data protection notice, declaration text, and terms are approved before public portal launch.
