## Administrator Handover

- Configure Embassy Settings before enabling the public portal.
- Replace placeholder branding with client-approved logo and emblem assets only.
- Create ERPNext Items for each payable fee and link them from Consular Service or Consular Fee Rule.
- Create Email Account and edit EMS email templates for each supported language.
- Review workflows with mission leadership before activation.

## Officer Handover

Officers work from Consular Application and Embassy Appointment queues. They should use Communication for applicant-facing messages and internal comments for private notes.

## Finance Handover

Finance officers should confirm bank transfer proofs in Consular Payment Review and reconcile against Sales Invoice and Payment Entry.
